Examples¶

Nengo creates these models using the principles of the Neural Engineering Framework. The first set of examples explains these three principles: representation, transformation, and dynamics.

For a summary of these principles, see the following example:

  • Principle 1: Representation
  • Principle 2: Transformation
  • Principle 3: Dynamics
